About Interlake Rocketry Club

🚀

Mission

To inspire Interlake students to become the next generation of aerospace engineers through hands-on rocketry projects and STEM education.

🎯

Vision

Building rockets that reach new heights while building skills that last a lifetime at Interlake High School.

🌟

Values

Innovation, teamwork, safety, and a passion for exploration drive everything we do as Interlake Saints.

Our Projects

📡

Weather Balloon Mission

Launching high-altitude balloons from Interlake's campus to collect atmospheric data and capture stunning views of the Pacific Northwest.

🎯

Model Rocket Competition

Designing and launching model rockets for regional and national competitions, representing Interlake High School.

🔬

Research & Development

Experimenting with new propulsion systems and materials for improved rocket performance in our school's science labs.
